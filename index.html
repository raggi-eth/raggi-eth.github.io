How it works

Our platform is built on the Polygon blockchain, which means that it is completely decentralized and transparent. This means that creators have complete control over their content and how it is shared with their audience. It also means that patrons have complete control over how they support their favorite creators.

Creators can use our platform to crowdfund their creative projects and deliver the finished product to their supporters. It's also like a Patreon site, where creators can give their fans early or exclusive access to their content. Or, if you're a creator looking to monetize your content, you can sell access to your premium content to your most dedicated fans and followers.

One key feature of our platform is that it is powered by a single minting contract. This means that all of the creative projects and exclusive unlockable content on the platform are secured and delivered through this contract. This ensures that the distribution and access to content is fair and transparent for both creators and patrons.

In addition, the platform uses the darkblock protocol to secure and deliver all of the exclusive content. This ensures that the content remains accessible only to those who have paid for access, while also allowing for easy delivery and management of the content. Overall, the combination of the single minting contract and the darkblock protocol helps to create a seamless and secure experience for both creators and patrons on the platform.

"Technical" Overview

At the core of our platform is a super cool shared ERC1155 minting contract. This contract lets us create multiple types of tokens within a single contract, which means we can make all sorts of NFTs for different parts of the platform. Plus, the ERC1155 standard also lets us transfer a bunch of different token types in just one transaction. That's way more efficient than other NFT standards!

But wait, there's more! We also have an API that manages everything related to minting from the contract, including all the metadata for each token id. The base_uri of the minting contract points to a route in the API, which fetches the right metadata for each id based on the contract address, token id, and flags.

Now for the really cool part: our exclusive platform content. We use the Darkblock protocol to add unlockable content to NFTs. This protocol encrypts the content and links it to a specific NFT or multiple NFTs, so only NFT holders can access the content. Neat, right?

We have four types of NFTs on the platform:

  • Profile NFT: this represents a user's profile on the platform. Any user can mint one for free, and minting someone else's profile means you're following them on the platform. Profile NFTs make it easy to find and follow other users, and they also help verify users' identities. The unlimited supply of these NFTs means anyone can follow as many users as they want, making it easy to build a community on the platform.
  • Post NFT: this represents a post to the communal feed. Like the profile NFT, these are free to mint and minting a post NFT means you like it. The unlimited supply of post NFTs lets users engage with and show support for the content on the platform. Plus, the ability to switch between chronological, followed users, or most liked posts feeds lets users customize their experience on the platform.

In summary, NFTs on our platform offer a unique and interactive experience for both creators and users. Creators can involve their communities in the distribution and promotion of their content, and users can show support for their favorite creators and curate their own feeds based on their interests. Cool, huh?




  • Content Container NFT: this is where creators store their exclusive content. The supply of these NFTs can be either unlimited or limited, depending on the creator's settings when they set up the token metadata. Minting a content container NFT gives you permanent access to the exclusive content and the ability to deploy access NFTs for that content. This lets creators involve their communities in the distribution of their content and lets other users become curators on the platform.
  • Access NFT: this represents mutable access to a creator's exclusive content. Access can be granted on a per-view basis or for a set period of time, depending on the creator's preferences. Minting an access NFT lets you view the exclusive content for the agreed-upon time. The proceeds from the sale of access NFTs are shared between the collector, creator, and the platform, providing a way for creators to monetize their content and for users to support their favorite creators.
